# 🐱 Cat Desktop Companion

Our Desktop Companion is a cute, interactive desktop assistant designed to help you stay productive. It sits on your desktop, tracks your mouse with its eyes, reacts to your typing, and helps you manage your time with an integrated Pomodoro timer.

## ✨ Features
* **Procedural Animation:** A custom-drawn cat that follows your mouse and reacts to your keyboard.
* **Pomodoro Focus Timer:** Integrated focus sessions (15m, 25m, 50m) displayed directly on the companion's desk.
* **Customizable Themes:** Switch between Midnight Dark, Ginger Tabby, and Snow White.
* **Right-Click Interaction:** Full control via a slick, integrated context menu.
* **Stay-on-Top:** Always visible, non-intrusive companion.
